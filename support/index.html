Common questions

  • How do I play? Open the app and tap How to Play from the main menu — it walks through the rules.
  • I bought a deck pack but it’s locked. Make sure you’re signed in to the same Apple ID used for the purchase, then open the deck in Deck Catalog and tap Restore Purchases.
  • Can I change the language? Yes — Settings → Language (English and Turkish are supported).
  • New decks? Decks are delivered over the air, so new content can appear without an app update.
